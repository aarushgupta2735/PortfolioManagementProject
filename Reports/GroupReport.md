## Company Overview

**Axis Bank** is one of India's leading private sector banks with over 30 years of operations. Its ethos — *"dil se open"* — reflects its commitment to openness and accessibility. The bank offers a wide range of services including retail and wholesale banking, wealth management, and digital banking solutions.

### Business Segments

| Segment | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Retail Banking** | Savings/current accounts, loans, credit cards, wealth management |
| **Wholesale Banking** | Working capital loans, project finance, trade services for corporates |
| **Treasury Operations** | Investment portfolio management, forex, derivatives |
| **Digital & Tech Services** | Mobile banking, internet banking, AI/ML-driven customer experiences |

### Strategic Focus
- Digital transformation via AI, big data, and machine learning
- ESG integration and sustainable finance
- Expanding urban and rural reach through technology
